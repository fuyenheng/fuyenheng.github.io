LKIPS Championship Stage 3 Finals Champion

Kaspersky Interactive Protection Simulation (KIPS) is an exercise that places business decision makers IT security teams from corporations and government departments into a simulated business environment facing a series of unexpected cyber threats, while trying to maximize profit and maintain confidence. As a team member of MH 4/2, we fought against 100+ international teams across the world for 3 stages. Each stage has unique environment yet realistic choices and consequences. In the end, we managed to win through all stages, aquired the highest points among the team and become champion.
