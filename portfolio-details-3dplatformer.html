3D Platformer Treasure Hunter

Treasure Hunter is a Platformer game where your role is to traverse the undiscovered temple in search of untold treasure. But you must be careful,for danger lurks every corner. The Undead roams the temple to safeguard the treasure, dangerous traps hidden all around the temple. The temple will be sure to keep you on your toes at all times. What you do matters in the game,as there are multiple endings in the game, so make your actions count and be mindful of the rules of the temple.
